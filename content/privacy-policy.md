Privacy Policy

The App does not collect any private or personally identifiable information from users. The only information collected pertains to the time-related data necessary for the functioning of the App's features. Rest assured, user privacy and data security are of utmost importance to us, and all collected data is handled in compliance with relevant privacy regulations and industry best practices.

How We Use Information
The time-related information collected by the App is used solely for the purpose of providing its intended features and functionality. This information is used internally and is not shared with any third parties.

Data Security

Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.
